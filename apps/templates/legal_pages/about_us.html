About us

About Us

Welcome to PriceTrackerWeb, your go-to destination for tracking product prices, monitoring stock availability, and discovering great deals on popular online platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra. At PriceTrackerWeb, we strive to make online shopping a seamless and rewarding experience for our users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower consumers with the tools and information they need to make informed purchasing decisions. We understand the importance of finding the best deals and ensuring product availability, which is why we've created a comprehensive platform that keeps you updated on price fluctuations, stock statuses, and special offers.

What We Offer

  • Price Tracking: We monitor the prices of products across various online retailers, including Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra. Our advanced tracking system notifies you instantly when prices drop or increase, helping you save money on your favorite products.
  • Stock Availability: Never miss out on a product again! We keep track of stock availability and notify you as soon as a product becomes available or goes out of stock. Say goodbye to disappointment when your desired item is suddenly unavailable.
  • Price History: Want to know how the price of a product has changed over time? Our price history feature provides you with detailed insights into past price fluctuations, allowing you to make informed decisions about when to buy.
  • Deal Recommendations: Discover the best deals and offers on Amazon and Flipkart with our deal recommendation system. We handpick the most attractive discounts and promotions, ensuring that you never miss out on a great deal.

How It Works

  1. Track Products: Simply provide us with the product details, and we'll start tracking its price and availability across multiple online platforms.
  2. Receive Notifications: Receive real-time notifications via email or SMS whenever there's a price drop, price increase, or change in stock status for your tracked products.
  3. Explore Price History: Dive into the price history of your favorite products to understand pricing trends and make smarter purchasing decisions.
  4. Discover Deals: Browse through our curated list of deals and offers to find incredible discounts on a wide range of products.

Why Choose Us?

  • Accuracy: Our tracking system is highly accurate, ensuring that you receive timely updates on price changes and stock availability.
  • Convenience: We make online shopping hassle-free by providing all the information you need in one place, saving you time and effort.
  • Transparency: We believe in transparency and honesty, which is why we strive to provide clear and comprehensive information to our users.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your satisfaction is our top priority. We're committed to delivering exceptional service and helping you make the most of your online shopping experience.
